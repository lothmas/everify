About E - Qualification Authority

E - Qualification Authority (E - Verify) is a central qualification repository authority which is affiliated to both local and E - examination bodies.

What we do?

The system distributes examination results and is a central repository which holds candidates results. In the process the system links potential candidates to employees and businesses.

Examination Bodies

E - Verify gets results from examination bodies or from candidates providing original certificates and transcripts which are evaluated and authenticated by E - Verify . Collected information also includes students contact details

Results Distribution

The second component of the system is to distribute results to awaiting candidates via sms, email, or the students can visit the online platform where they can log-onto the system to view their results.

Third-Parties

Third-Parties can be given approval by the candidate to access their results.

The system can link potential candidates to employers looking to fill-in available posts.
